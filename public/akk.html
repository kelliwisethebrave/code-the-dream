Alaskan Klee Kai

The Alaskan Klee Kai is a new breed, created starting in the 1970s to be a miniature version of the larger arctic breeds. There are three sizes: toy (13 inches tall or fewer), miniature (over 13 inches tall up to 15 inches tall), and standard (more than 15 inches tall). The breed is fully recognized by the United Kennel Club, and on the way to full recognition by the American Kennel Club (currently in the Miscellaneous category).
